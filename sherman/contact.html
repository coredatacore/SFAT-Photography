Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for portrait sessions and 6-12 months for weddings and major events to ensure availability.

What is your cancellation policy?

Deposits are non-refundable, but we offer rescheduling with at least 48 hours notice at no additional charge.

Do you travel for shoots?

Yes, we are available for travel both domestically and internationally. Travel fees may apply depending on the location.

How long until I receive my photos/videos?

Typical turnaround time is 2-3 weeks for portrait sessions and 4-6 weeks for weddings and events.
